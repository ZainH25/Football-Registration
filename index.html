Rules & Regulations

Player Verification Protocols

1. Eligibility

  • Tournament is open to players 22 years of age or below.
  • Players must carry a valid government-issued ID or college/school ID for age verification.
  • Any team found using an overage player will be disqualified.

2. Team Composition

  • 6 players on the field (5 outfield players + 1 goalkeeper).
  • Maximum 10 players can be registered per team.
  • Minimum 4 players are required to start a match.

3. Match Duration

  • League Stage: 20 minutes (10 minutes each half with a 2-minute halftime).
  • Semi-Finals & Final: 24 minutes (12 minutes each half with a 2-minute halftime).
  • Referee’s decision on time is final.

4. Kick-Off

  • Teams must report 15 minutes before kick-off.
  • A toss will decide the kick-off and choice of ends.

5. Substitutions

  • Rolling substitutions are allowed.
  • Substitutions can only be made when the ball is out of play and with the referee’s permission.

6. Offside Rule

  • No offside rule.

7. Restarts

  • Kick-ins replace throw-ins.
  • Goal kicks must be taken from inside the penalty area.
  • Opponents must stand at least 5 meters away during free kicks, corners, and kick-ins.

8. Fouls & Misconduct

  • Dangerous tackles, abusive language, and violent conduct will not be tolerated.
  • Slide tackles are not allowed (except the goalkeeper inside the penalty area if permitted by the referee).
  • Repeated fouls may result in disciplinary action.

9. Cards

  • Yellow Card: Warning.
  • Two Yellow Cards: Red Card and suspension for the remainder of the match.
  • Red Card: Immediate dismissal. Team plays with one player fewer for 2 minutes or until the opposition scores, whichever comes first.
  • Serious misconduct may lead to suspension from the tournament.

10. Goalkeeper Rules

  • Goalkeeper may use hands only inside the penalty area.
  • Goalkeeper cannot pick up a deliberate back-pass from a teammate.

11. Points System

  • Win – 3 points
  • Draw – 1 point
  • Loss – 0 points

12. League Stage Tie-Breakers

If teams finish level on points:

  1. Goal Difference
  2. Goals Scored
  3. Head-to-Head Result
  4. Fair Play Record
  5. Penalty Shootout (if required)

13. Knockout Matches

  • If tied at full time, the match goes directly to a Penalty Shootout.
  • Each team takes 3 penalties.
  • Sudden death follows if still tied.

14. Walkover

  • Teams arriving 10 minutes late after the scheduled kick-off time will forfeit the match.
  • Walkover score: 3–0.

15. Equipment

  • Shin guards are recommended.
  • Players must wear proper football shoes suitable for turf.
  • No metal studs.
  • Teams should wear matching jerseys or bibs.

16. Referee’s Decision

  • The referee’s decision is final and binding.
  • No arguments or abusive behavior towards match officials will be tolerated.

17. Discipline

  • Any player or team involved in fighting, abuse, or misconduct may be disqualified without a refund.
  • Alcohol, drugs, or smoking on the playing area are strictly prohibited.

18. Organizer’s Rights

  • Tournament organizers reserve the right to modify the schedule due to weather or unforeseen circumstances.
  • Any issue not covered in these rules will be decided by the organizing committee.

FAIR PLAY: Respect opponents, referees, and organizers. Play with sportsmanship and enjoy the game.
